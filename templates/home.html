BPSC (BIHAR PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION) PREPRATION HUB:

bpsc short details:

The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) conducts the BPSC Examination to recruit candidates for various administrative positions in the state government of Bihar, such as Bihar Administrative Service, Bihar Police Service, and more. The exam consists of three stages: Preliminary, Mains, and Interview, designed to test candidates' knowledge, aptitude, and suitability for public service. It plays a key role in selecting competent individuals for governance in Bihar.

BIHAR SI (BIHAR SUB-INSPECTOR) PREPRATION HUB:

bihar sub-inspector short details:

The Bihar Sub-Inspector(SI) Exam is conducted by the Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) to recruit candidates for the position of Sub-Inspector in the Bihar Police. The selection process includes a Preliminary exam, Mains exam, Physical Efficiency Test (PET), and Medical Examination. It is designed to assess candidates' knowledge, physical fitness, and aptitude for law enforcement roles in the state.
